Schnitzel

Description

Easy recipy for a fingerlicking schnitzel

Try to make it on your own!
Prepare ingredients and follow instructions.

  • One chicken breast
  • Fresh lemon
  • Bread crumbs
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Huge potato
  • Ketchup (optional)
  1. Cut the breast in half and open wide forming one piece of meat.
  2. On two plates put beaten salted egg and bread crumbs.
  3. Dump meat into egg and then cover it with bread crumbs. Press down so it will stick thight.
  4. Pour oil onto the pan and turn the heat. Once it's hot turn the heat down a little bit and put schnitzels on pan.
  5. Fry till golden brown and crispy. Once they're done cover them with aluminium foil for few minutes.
  6. Peel potato and cut into fries. Wash them well.
  7. Pour huge dose of oil into the pan (or you can also use pot) and once its hot dump the fries.
  8. Wait till golden (should take them around 6 to 8 minutes). Take them out of pot. Put on the paper towel and season with salt.
  9. Put everything together and give the schnitzel few drops od lemon juice.
  10. Well done! Enjoy your meal.
